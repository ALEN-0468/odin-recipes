Discription

This south india's special dish and all having differents types of biriyanis in different states of south part of india.

Ingredients

  • Biriyani rice
  • chicken
  • Onion,ginger
  • ghee
  • Fresh mint

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Slice onions thinly, chop coriander and mint leaves, prepare ginger-garlic paste, and gather whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves).
  2. Marinate Meat: Mix chicken pieces (bone-in recommended for flavor) with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, biryani masala, lemon juice, salt, and half of the fried onions.
  3. Rest: Let the meat marinate for at least 30 minutes, or 4–24 hours in the refrigerator for deeper flavor.
  4. Soak Rice: Rinse 500g of aged basmati rice 3–4 times until the water runs clear, then soak for 30 minutes.
  5. Fry Onions (Birista): Heat ghee or oil in a heavy-bottomed pot and fry the sliced onions until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Parboil Rice: Bring a large pot of water to a boil, adding whole spices (cardamom, cloves, bay leaf, cumin) and salt. Add the soaked rice and cook until it is 70-80% done (should have a slight bite).
  7. Drain: Drain the rice immediately in a colander.
  8. Layer: Spread the partially cooked chicken evenly at the bottom of the pot. Layer the parboiled rice over the chicken.
  9. Rest: Turn off the heat and let the biryani rest for 15-20 minutes before serving to allow the rice to settle.
