Description

This is a tradional kerala dish making from using flour,water and spices

Ingredients

  • flour - 2 cups
  • Water - 3/4 cup
  • Sugar - 1 tsp
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  • Oil - 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Mix Ingredients: In a large bowl, mix 3 cups of refined flour (maida), 1 tsp salt, 1 tbsp sugar, and 2 tbsp ghee or oil. Some recipes suggest adding an egg or 1/4 cup of milk for extra softness.
  2. Knead: Slowly add water to the mixture and knead well to form a soft, pliable dough. Knead for about 5–10 minutes, pushing the dough with your thumb, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Initial Rest: Apply oil (approx. 2 tbsp) all over the dough to prevent drying. Cover with a damp cloth or lid and let it rest for at least 1 to 2 hours.
  4. Divide and Roll: After resting, punch down the dough and knead again. Divide the dough into equal-sized small balls (beeda).
  5. Second Rest: Coat each ball with oil and allow them to rest for another 15–20 minutes.
